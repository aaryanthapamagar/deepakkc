Hack Club Butwal

Hack Club Butwal is a vibrant community of young innovators, programmers, designers, and tech enthusiasts. We come together to learn, create, and share knowledge in the world of technology. Our goal is to inspire creativity, promote teamwork, and help students develop real-world problem-solving skills.

We organize workshops, coding events, hackathons, and collaborative projects where everyone can learn from each other. Whether you are a beginner or an experienced coder, Hack Club Butwal welcomes you to be part of our journey to build a better future through technology and innovation.
